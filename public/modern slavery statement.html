Modern Slavery Statement

Introduction

AXH Media unequivocally denounces all forms of slavery, human trafficking, and forced labor. We are dedicated to ensuring that our business operations, along with our global supply chains, are free from any sort of modern slavery or exploitation. Our commitment extends to all our employees, contractors, clients, partners, and users.

Scope

This Modern Slavery Statement applies to all entities within AXH Media and is relevant to all our employees, suppliers, partners, and contractors. This is in accordance with the Modern Slavery Act of 2015 and is valid for the financial year ending in 2023.

Policies and Contractual Controls

We have put in place a variety of policies that ensure that we comply with all relevant legislation, and more importantly, maintain our commitment to human rights, dignity, and fairness. Our Employee Code of Conduct outlines the behavior we expect from all our staff, promoting ethical practices, mutual respect, and the assurance of a safe and healthy work environment.

Our Supplier Code of Conduct expects similar standards from all our suppliers, especially those providing freelance content creation, digital design services, social media monitoring tools, or other digital products and services. We require our partners to confirm their adherence to our code and to national and international laws related to labor rights.

Due Diligence and Risk Assessment

We understand that sectors involving digital labor, such as social media marketing, have the potential to harbor hidden forms of modern slavery. Therefore, AXH Media takes rigorous measures to identify and mitigate this risk.

We conduct a thorough due diligence process before partnering with any third-party agencies, influencers, freelancers, or data providers. This involves checking their own modern slavery statements, compliance with labor laws, and overall ethical conduct.

Our risk assessment process also takes into consideration the geographical regions of our partners. Regions with high instances of human rights abuses are flagged, and business engagements in these areas are strictly scrutinized or avoided altogether.

Training

At AXH Media, we believe that awareness and education are key to combating modern slavery. All our employees receive regular training on modern slavery, human trafficking, and labor rights. We ensure they can recognize potential instances of modern slavery and understand the proper course of action if such issues arise.

Reporting and Whistleblowing

We encourage our employees and partners to report any concerns about modern slavery through our Whistleblowing Policy. This policy protects whistleblowers from any form of retaliation and ensures that all reported instances are thoroughly investigated.

Conclusion

At AXH Media, we believe in an ethical, fair, and transparent digital world. We are fully committed to combating modern slavery in all its forms and will continually assess our practices to ensure they remain effective.

This statement was approved by our Board of Directors and will be reviewed annually to maintain its relevance and effectiveness.
